Mastering Excellence: CHCCI Unveiling Study Habits

"Diving deep into academic excellence at Concepcion Holy Cross College!"

Today marked a transformative moment as the School of Nursing orchestrated the enlightening seminar, 'Mastering Excellence: CHCCI Unveiling Study Habits' on January 15, 2024.

A highlight of the event was Ms. Virginia Marie P. Bautista, our esteemed guest speaker, who generously shared her wealth of wisdom on cultivating effective study habits. Her insights are sure to become guiding lights for our students navigating the academic landscape.

This seminar, a collaborative effort of each department officers, underscores CHCCI's commitment to nurturing a culture of continuous improvement. The primary goal is to elevate the academic outputs of our students, empowering them to strive for the excellence they truly deserve.

In embracing the ethos of 'Study Smart,' these initiatives aim to propel our students toward not just satisfactory but outstanding performance, paving the way for a rewarding semester ahead. A heartfelt salute to all the organizers for their dedication to fostering an environment where intellectual growth thrives!
